What is this?

-Smigo is a garden planner for vegetables and flowers. It will give you advice about companion planting and crop rotation.

How does it work?

-First you need to signup and login. Click on a species on the left menu and then somewhere in the grid. Now you have added your first plant. Add more species and items so your grid equals your garden.

How do I delete a plant?

-Hold shift and click square or select the delete plant and click the square.

Is it for free?
